WHY TMUX

tmux is useful in different ways. To me, its most useful as a way to maintain various windows and panels in a single terminal window. And also to maitain peristent working states on remote servers, allowing you to detach and re-attach at will.

WHAT ABOUT SCREEN?

tmux is a lot like screen, only better. Few advantages of tmux over screen: - Tmux supports both emacs and vim shortcuts - Tmux support auto-renaming windows - Tmux is highly scriptable - Window splitting is more advanced in tmux
